#+title: Cocktail Ice Reference

* Ice
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: cocktail-ice
:END:

** No Ice (Up / Straight)
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: ice-up
:END:
Cold, no ongoing dilution. Requires chilled glass.

** Cubed Ice
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: ice-cubed
:END:
Standard bar ice. Balanced dilution.

** Large Cube / Sphere
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: ice-large
:END:
Slow dilution, spirit-forward drinks.

** Cracked Ice
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: ice-cracked
:END:
Fast chilling, moderate dilution.

** Crushed Ice
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: ice-crushed
:END:
Maximum dilution, frosted glass.

** Pebble / Nugget Ice
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: ice-pebble
:END:
Soft chew, fast chilling.
